## Ayala Land plans P50-billion fundraising in second half
Ayala Land Inc. (ALI) plans to raise P50 billion in the second half of 2025, with a focus on sustainability-linked financing. The property developer is betting on anticipated interest rate cuts to finance maturing obligations and new residential projects, including a significant launch for its core segment.
