## Factbox-Chinese-owned solar factories in the United States
Illuminate is a joint venture between Chinese solar equipment maker Longi and U.S. clean energy project developer Invenergy. The company started producing solar panels at a five-gigawatt plant in Pataskala, Ohio in February. Illuminate said it is investing $600 million in equipment, raw materials, salaries and staff training and is evaluating whether to expand into cell manufacturing.
